About DisPredict

DisPredict is a machine learning-powered web application designed to assist users in predicting potential diseases based on their symptoms. By leveraging a Random Forest model trained on medical datasets, the application provides accurate predictions and helpful recommendations.

Simply enter your symptoms in the input field, and DisPredict will analyze the data to predict the most likely disease. Along with the prediction, the app provides a detailed description of the disease, precautions to take, recommended medications, workout suggestions, and dietary advice to help you manage your health effectively.

This project is developed by Vishal, Suresh, Naveen, and RajKumar as part of their MCA coursework. The goal is to make healthcare more accessible and empower users with valuable insights into their health.
